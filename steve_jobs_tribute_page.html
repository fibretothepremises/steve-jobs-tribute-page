My impressions of Steve Jobs

Anyone can pull up facts and figures from Steve's wiki page so let me list my own impressions of Steve Jobs and why I chose to make a tribute page for him.

Things I like(d) about Steve Jobsy

  • The passion he brings to the stage when presenting his new products
  • His ENERGY!
  • His products!
  • His simple dress - black T-shirt and jeans. There's something about it that I love
  • The way he spoke and particularly the way he emphasised certain words to make a point
  • The PASSION in his voice
  • The sense of authority he brings with him
  • His keynote presentations (which derives all the qualities I find in him above)
  • His general charisma and charm
  • His sense of humour

If you do want to know more about Steve Jobs - his history and background, achievements and what not - head over to his Wikpedia page and read all about it!
